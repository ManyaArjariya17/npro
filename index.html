Here are some Top places to visit!!!

Badi Lake

Badi Lake Udaipur is a generally lesser-known lovely lake situated around 12 kilometers from Udaipur City, a much-needed reprieve from the packed city.

Udaipur Fish Aquarium

Under The Sun is the biggest public aquarium display regarding gallons of water, numbers, and types of fish in the whole Nation. The aquarium is a 125-meter-long display that has various fishes.


City Palace Museum

City Palace is main tourist attraction in udaipur situated over Lake Pichola.Inside city palace complex there is 2 hotels and one crystal gallery for tourist.

Bharatiya Lok Kala Mandal

Established in 1983. It is functioning since then to conserve and showcase Folk Art.Culture, Songs and Festivals of Rajasthan, Gujarat and Madhya Pradesh.


Jagmandir & Pichhola

The smaller of the two artificial lakes in Udaipur city, the Lake Pichhola is famous for being surrounded by dense green hills and woodlands.

Shilpgram

The Shilpgram offers a look into a cultural heritage of rural arts and crafts which is a study in craftsmanship, design aesthetics and traditions.
